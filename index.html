Nebula ScoreWorks - Music analyser that runs in your browser

Drop a piece of digital sheet music in and the system breaks it down automatically: what key it's in, every chord, the song's structure (verse / chorus / bridge), and where the tension rises and falls. Renders the score visually as you scroll. No server, no install.

Music key-detection engine

The "brain" inside the analyser. Figures out what key a piece of music is in (out of 24 possibilities), then breaks it into individual chords. Lifted accuracy from 37 % to 81.85 % on a public 909-piece test set, scored using the same method professional music-research teams use.
